着
zhe(5) • zháo(2)
Note: zhe (neutral tone) is most common, zháo is used in specific compounds

Common Usages

Progressive Action (正在)

他正在看着书。
Tā zhèngzài kàn zhe shū.
He is reading a book.

Continuous State

门开着。
Mén kāi zhe.
The door is open (remains open).

Emphasis (着重)

着重说明
zhuózhòng shuōmíng
Emphasize; stress
